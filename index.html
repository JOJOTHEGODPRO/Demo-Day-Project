Famous Players

Soccer is a worldwide sport and due to this fact, there are many players known worldwide for excelling at the sport of soccer. One of my favorite soccer players is Cristiano Ronaldo. Cristiano Ronaldo, known as Ronaldo, is widely known as one of the best players in soccer. This is due to his incredible physique and unbelievable speed and dribbling technique. With these great strengths, he and his team have won the UEFA European Champonship, a very competetive soccer tournament, and he has been awarded 5 Ballon d'Or, one of soccers highest aknowledgments of achievement. This is why even at 39, he is still recognized as an incredible player. Another great and famous player is Lionel Messi. Lionel Messi, known as Messi is also a world renowned player of soccer. He also has amazing speed and dribbling technique. However, one of his greatest strengths is his ability to work with and improve his team. With this, he has won the world cup, the most competetive and largest competition within soccer, and he has also been awarded the Ballon d'Or. These are two amazing players, but there are still millions of great players within the sport of soccer.
